# Customer Journey Map

## Aware
**Trigger Event:** Developer encounters legacy code or complex codebase that is difficult to maintain.
**Friction Points:** Lack of awareness about refactoring tools, time constraints, and perceived complexity of refactoring.
**User Emotions:** Frustration, overwhelm, and uncertainty.
**Opportunities to Delight:** Educational content (blogs, webinars) on the importance of refactoring and how AI can assist.
**Metric:** Number of unique visitors to educational content.

## Consider
**Trigger Event:** Developer searches for solutions to improve code maintainability and efficiency.
**Friction Points:** Overwhelming number of options, skepticism about AI tools, and cost concerns.
**User Emotions:** Hopeful but cautious, seeking reliable solutions.
**Opportunities to Delight:** Case studies, testimonials, and free trials showcasing the effectiveness of RefactorAI.
**Metric:** Number of downloads of whitepapers or case studies.

## Try
**Trigger Event:** Developer signs up for a free trial or demo of RefactorAI.
**Friction Points:** Complex setup process, lack of integration with existing tools, and initial learning curve.
**User Emotions:** Excitement mixed with anxiety about the tool's effectiveness.
**Opportunities to Delight:** Seamless onboarding process, intuitive UI, and quick setup with popular IDEs and version control systems.
**Metric:** Conversion rate from trial sign-ups to active users.

## Adopt
**Trigger Event:** Developer experiences tangible benefits from using RefactorAI, such as improved code quality and reduced maintenance time.
**Friction Points:** Cost of subscription, resistance to change from team members, and integration challenges.
**User Emotions:** Satisfaction, relief, and confidence in the tool's capabilities.
**Opportunities to Delight:** Personalized support, regular updates, and community forums for sharing best practices.
**Metric:** Percentage of trial users who convert to paid subscribers.

## Expand
**Trigger Event:** Developer shares positive experiences with colleagues and other teams within the organization.
**Friction Points:** Organizational approval processes, budget constraints, and scaling challenges.
**User Emotions:** Pride in contributing to improved code quality, enthusiasm for expanding the tool's use.
**Opportunities to Delight:** Enterprise-level support, custom integrations, and bulk licensing options.
**Metric:** Number of additional licenses purchased within the same organization.
