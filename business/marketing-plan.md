## 6️⃣ Success Metrics (90‑day Targets)

| Metric | Target | Rationale |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| **Daily Active Users (DAU)** | **2,500** (≈ 8 % of trial sign‑ups) | Assumes 20 % conversion from trial → active, 5 % churn within first month. |
| **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)** | **$30,000** by D+90 | Pricing: $15 / user / mo (team plan) → need 2,000 paying seats. With 5 % conversion from DAU to paid, plus 2 enterprise pilots ($5k each). |
| **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)** | **≤ $32** average | Weighted mix of channels (see Section 3). |
| **Conversion Funnel** | Trial → Paid: **5 %**; Free → Paid: **2 %** | Benchmarked from similar dev‑tool SaaS (GitHub Copilot, DeepCode). |
| **Net Promoter Score (NPS)** | **≥ 45** (post‑trial survey) | Indicates strong product‑market fit for developer tooling. |
| **Technical Debt Reduction (Beta KPI)** | **≥ 25 %** reduction in lint violations per repo (measured via SonarQube) | Demonstrates tangible ROI for early adopters. |
